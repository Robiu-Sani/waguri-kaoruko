Rintaro Tsumugi

The world sees a delinquent, but she sees the boy who saves the last piece of cake for his mother. Rintaro's journey is one of shedding the armor he built to survive. Every interaction with Waguri is a petal falling from the wall he built around his heart. He discovers that dignity isn't about looking tough—it's about the courage to be gentle when the world expects you to be a storm.

His silence isn't anger; it's the quiet of someone who hasn't been heard for a long time. Through the scent of the flowers at Kikyo, he finally finds his own voice.

Kaoruko Waguri

She is the fragrance that lingers long after the flower has bloomed. Kaoruko doesn't just look at people; she perceives them. Her kindness isn't a weakness; it is a conscious choice she makes every single day to bridge the gap between their rival schools.

In her eyes, there are no "bad boys" or "perfect girls"—only individuals waiting to be understood. Her love for Rintaro is pure, unwavering, and acts as the light that guides him home.
